Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

Pay & Compensation

Payday is every Friday (weekly). Pay is deposited directly to your bank account. If Friday falls on a holiday, you'll be paid the day before.
Tips belong to the crew who earned them. The crew lead handles distribution at the job site according to role-specific guidelines — see your compensation doc for details. All tips must be reported for tax purposes.
Pay reviews happen annually in January. Raises are based on performance, customer feedback, reliability, and skill development. Talk to your supervisor about what you need to do to advance to the next pay grade.
Several! Perfect attendance ($100/month), 5-star reviews that mention you by name ($25), referral bonus ($200), peak season bonus (+$1/hr May-Sept), and zero damage bonus ($50/month). Check your role-specific compensation doc for full details.

Time Off & Scheduling

Submit PTO requests through the scheduling system at least 2 weeks in advance. During peak season (May-September), requests may be limited, so plan ahead. For emergencies, call the office as soon as possible.
Call the office AND your crew lead as early as possible—ideally before 6 AM. Use your accrued sick time. If you're contagious, stay home! We'd rather find coverage than have the whole crew get sick.
Find a coworker willing to cover your shift, then both of you need to notify dispatch for approval. Swaps must be confirmed at least 24 hours before the shift. You're still responsible if your replacement doesn't show.
