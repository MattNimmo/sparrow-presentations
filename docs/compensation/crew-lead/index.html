Crew Lead Compensation

Crew Lead

This document outlines the complete compensation structure for Crew Leads at Sparrow Moving, including hourly rates, leadership pay, tips, and bonus opportunities.

Hourly Pay

Pay Grades

Crew Lead pay reflects leadership responsibility and experience. Rates are reviewed annually — increases are based on grade, performance, and tenure.

Crew Lead I $25/hr
Crew Lead II $26 – $27/hr

Grade Progression

  • CL I → II: 1 year as lead + positive crew feedback + no major incidents (at manager's discretion)

Overtime

All hours over 40 per week are paid at 1.5x your regular rate.

Tips

Tip Distribution

As crew lead, you distribute tips at the end of each job:

  • 2-person crew: 50/50 split with your mover
  • 3-person crew: Equal thirds
  • 4+ person crew: Equal shares to all movers
Lead by example: Crew leads take the same share as movers. Fair tip distribution builds trust and crew morale.

Tip Expectations

  • Tips are earned by the whole crew's performance
  • Never discuss tips with customers
  • Distribute tips transparently in front of the crew
  • All tips must be reported for tax purposes

Vehicle Responsibility Pay

DOT Compliance Bonus

Crew leads who maintain perfect vehicle inspection records and DOT compliance receive an additional $50/month.

Out of State Moves

When a job requires travel out of state, you are compensated at a day rate rather than an hourly rate.

How the Day Rate Works

Your day rate is calculated as:

Day Rate = Hourly Wage × 12 hours

For example: if your hourly wage is $22/hr, your day rate for an out-of-state move is $264.

  • Any job where the origin or destination is outside of Minnesota
  • Day rate applies to each full day of travel or work on the job
  • Questions about a specific job's classification? Contact the office before the job date

Sample Pay Scenarios

Example: Crew Lead II, Strong Month

  • 180 hours worked @ $26/hr (some OT)
  • Regular: 160 hrs × $26 = $4,160
  • Overtime: 20 hrs × $39 = $780
  • Tips (20 jobs): ~$1,600
  • Monthly Total: ~$6,540

Pay Day

  • Frequency: Weekly (every Friday)
  • Method: Direct deposit (required)
  • Pay stubs: Available in employee portal
