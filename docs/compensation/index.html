General Compensation Policies

Pay Schedule

All employees are paid weekly on Fridays via direct deposit.

Overtime

Non-exempt employees earn 1.5x their regular rate for hours over 40 per workweek. Overtime must be pre-approved when possible.

Tip Policy

Tips are the property of the crew who earned them. Distribution is handled at the job site by the crew lead according to role-specific guidelines.

Pay Increases

Pay reviews happen annually in January. Increases are based on:

  • Performance and reliability
  • Customer feedback
  • Skill development
  • Tenure with the company
