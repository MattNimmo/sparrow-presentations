Mover Compensation

Mover

This document outlines the complete compensation structure for Movers at Sparrow Moving, including hourly rates, tips, and bonus opportunities.

Hourly Pay

Pay Grades

Mover pay is based on experience and performance. Rates are reviewed annually — increases are based on grade, performance, and tenure.

Mover I (Entry) $20 – $21/hr
Mover II (Experienced) $21 – $22/hr
Mover III (Senior) $23 – $24/hr

Grade Progression

  • Mover I → II: 90 days + performance review + crew lead recommendation
  • Mover II → III: 2 years + demonstrated leadership + specialty skills

Overtime

All hours over 40 per week are paid at 1.5x your regular rate.

Tips

Tip Distribution

Tips are distributed at the end of each job by the crew lead:

  • 2-person crew: 50/50 split between movers (crew lead gets same as mover)
  • 3-person crew: Equal thirds
  • 4+ person crew: Equal shares to all movers present
Average tips: Movers typically earn $50-150 per day in tips depending on job size and customer satisfaction. Your attitude and effort directly impact your tips!

Tip Expectations

  • Tips are earned, not guaranteed
  • Quality work and positive attitude = better tips
  • All tips must be reported for tax purposes

Out of State Moves

When a job requires travel out of state, you are compensated at a day rate rather than an hourly rate.

How the Day Rate Works

Your day rate is calculated as:

Day Rate = Hourly Wage × 12 hours

For example: if your hourly wage is $18/hr, your day rate for an out-of-state move is $216.

  • Any job where the origin or destination is outside of Minnesota
  • Day rate applies to each full day of travel or work on the job
  • Questions about a specific job's classification? Contact the office before the job date

Sample Pay Scenarios

Example: Mover I, Busy Week

  • 45 hours worked @ $20/hr
  • Regular: 40 hrs × $20 = $800
  • Overtime: 5 hrs × $30 = $150
  • Tips (5 jobs): ~$400
  • Total: ~$1,350

Pay Day

  • Frequency: Weekly (every Friday)
  • Method: Direct deposit (required)
  • Pay stubs: Available in employee portal
