Estimator Job Description

Estimator

Position Overview

The Estimator is responsible for conducting accurate in-home or virtual move surveys, calculating pricing, and presenting quotes to potential customers. This role combines technical assessment skills with consultative selling to help families and businesses plan successful relocations.

Reports to: Sales Manager
Classification: Full-time, Exempt
Schedule: Monday–Saturday, flexible hours based on customer availability

Primary Responsibilities

Move Assessment

  • Conduct thorough in-home surveys to inventory all items being moved
  • Perform virtual video estimates for customers who prefer remote consultations
  • Assess specialty items requiring additional handling (pianos, safes, antiques, artwork)
  • Evaluate access challenges at origin and destination (stairs, elevators, long carries, parking)
  • Document property conditions and potential obstacles
  • Take photos of specialty items and access points for crew preparation

Pricing & Quoting

  • Calculate accurate estimates using Sparrow's pricing matrix and inventory data
  • Apply appropriate rates for specialty services (packing, crating, storage)
  • Factor in access fees, long carry charges, and other situational costs
  • Present binding and non-binding estimate options with clear explanations
  • Explain valuation coverage options and help customers choose appropriate protection
  • Process deposits and signed agreements using company systems

Customer Consultation

  • Educate customers on the moving process and what to expect
  • Provide packing tips and recommendations for move-day preparation
  • Answer questions about timing, logistics, and services
  • Address concerns and build confidence in Sparrow's service quality
  • Follow up with customers who haven't yet booked after receiving estimates

Documentation & Handoff

  • Enter complete inventory data into Chariot CRM within 24 hours of survey
  • Document special instructions, customer preferences, and move-day notes
  • Flag high-risk items or situations for operations review
  • Participate in pre-move coordination meetings for complex jobs
  • Communicate with dispatch to ensure accurate crew and equipment allocation

Performance Metrics

Estimators are evaluated on accuracy, conversion, and customer satisfaction:

Key Performance Indicators

  • Estimate Accuracy: Actual move cost within 10% of estimate (target: 90%+ of jobs)
  • Conversion Rate: Percentage of estimates that book (target: 45%+)
  • Customer Rating: Post-estimate survey score (target: 4.5+ stars)
  • Response Time: Estimates delivered within 24 hours of survey
  • Documentation Quality: Complete and accurate CRM entries

Required Qualifications

  • High school diploma or equivalent; associate degree preferred
  • 2+ years experience in moving industry, home services sales, or related field
  • Valid driver's license with clean driving record
  • Strong mathematical aptitude for calculating estimates
  • Excellent verbal communication and presentation skills
  • Proficiency with tablets, smartphones, and CRM software
  • Professional appearance and demeanor

Preferred Qualifications

  • Experience with moving industry software (MoveBoard, Supermove, Chariot)
  • Bilingual (English/Spanish) strongly preferred
  • Background in sales, consulting, or customer service management
  • Knowledge of Minnesota housing market and neighborhoods
  • Previous experience with virtual/video estimate platforms

Essential Skills

Technical Knowledge

  • Understanding of move logistics, labor requirements, and timing
  • Knowledge of packing materials, techniques, and supply needs
  • Familiarity with specialty item handling requirements
  • Ability to assess cubic footage and weight estimates

Interpersonal Skills

  • Active listening to understand customer needs and concerns
  • Consultative approach to building trust and rapport
  • Clear explanation of complex pricing and service options
  • Professional handling of objections and negotiations
  • Empathy for the stress customers experience during moves

Organizational Skills

  • Self-management of appointment schedule and territory
  • Detailed documentation and follow-through
  • Prioritization of high-value opportunities
  • Timely communication with team members

Work Environment

  • Split between field work (customer homes/businesses) and office
  • Significant driving throughout the Twin Cities metro area
  • Must be comfortable entering various home environments
  • Occasional evening and weekend appointments based on customer availability
  • May require climbing stairs and navigating tight spaces during surveys

Compensation

See the Estimator Compensation Guide for complete details on salary, bonuses, and incentives.

Growth Opportunities

Successful Estimators may advance to:

  • Senior Estimator with expanded territory and mentoring responsibilities
  • Sales Manager overseeing the estimating team
  • Operations Manager with broader company leadership
  • Business development roles focusing on commercial accounts
