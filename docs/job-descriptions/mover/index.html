Mover

Mover

Position Summary

Movers are the backbone of Sparrow Moving. You'll work as part of a crew to safely pack, wrap, load, transport, and unload customer belongings. This is a physically demanding role that rewards hard workers with competitive pay, great tips, and opportunities for advancement.

Reports to: Crew Lead (on job site)
Supervises: None
Type: Full-time or Part-time, hourly

Key Responsibilities

Packing & Wrapping

  • Wrap furniture with moving blankets and stretch wrap
  • Pack boxes with customer items using proper techniques
  • Protect floors, walls, and banisters with runners and corner guards
  • Disassemble furniture when necessary (beds, tables, etc.)
  • Use appropriate materials for fragile and specialty items

Loading & Unloading

  • Carry items safely from home to truck and vice versa
  • Use dollies, hand trucks, and straps properly
  • Load truck strategically for safety and space efficiency
  • Secure items in truck using straps and load bars
  • Unload carefully and place items per customer direction

Customer Property Care

  • Handle all items with care regardless of apparent value
  • Alert crew lead to any pre-existing damage before moving
  • Follow specialty item procedures (TVs, mirrors, artwork)
  • Reassemble furniture at destination
  • Leave customer's home clean and organized

Team Collaboration

  • Follow crew lead's direction and job plan
  • Communicate with team members during lifts and maneuvers
  • Help maintain work pace to stay on schedule
  • Support teammates and ask for help when needed
  • Maintain positive attitude throughout the day

Safety & Equipment

  • Use proper lifting techniques (lift with legs, not back)
  • Wear appropriate footwear and work attire
  • Care for and return all equipment at end of shift
  • Report any injuries or safety hazards immediately
  • Follow all OSHA and company safety guidelines

Requirements

Required Qualifications

  • Must be 18 years or older
  • Ability to lift 75+ lbs repeatedly
  • Reliable transportation to warehouse
  • Valid ID for employment verification
  • Pass background check
  • Ability to work varied hours including early mornings and weekends

Preferred Qualifications

  • Previous moving experience
  • Valid driver's license
  • Bilingual (English/Spanish)
  • Experience with tools (basic assembly/disassembly)

Physical Requirements

  • Lift and carry heavy furniture (75-100 lbs solo, more with assistance)
  • Climb stairs repeatedly throughout shift (sometimes 3-4 floors)
  • Work in varying weather conditions (heat, cold, rain)
  • Stand, walk, bend, squat, and reach for extended periods
  • Work 8-12 hour shifts
Physical Demands: This is a physically demanding job. You'll be on your feet all day, climbing stairs, and lifting heavy items. Good physical condition is essential.

Skills & Competencies

Physical Stamina Essential
Teamwork Essential
Attention to Detail Essential
Reliability Essential
Positive Attitude Essential

Work Schedule

  • Days: Varies based on bookings
  • Hours: Start times vary (usually 6-8 AM), 8-12 hour days common
  • Weekends: Often required, especially during peak season
  • Peak Season: May-September, expect more hours and overtime

What We Provide

  • All equipment and supplies
  • Company uniform (shirts)
  • Training on proper techniques
  • Water and snacks on the truck
  • Supportive team environment

Growth Opportunities

Hard-working Movers can advance to:

  • Mover II (Senior Mover)
  • Mover III (Specialist)
  • Crew Lead
  • Driver (with CDL)
See also: Mover Compensation for pay details, tips, and bonus opportunities.
