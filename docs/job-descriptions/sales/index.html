Sales Representative

Sales

Position Summary

Sales Representatives are the first point of contact for potential customers. You'll respond to leads, build relationships, quote pricing, overcome objections, and book moves. Success in this role requires excellent communication skills, attention to detail, and a genuine desire to help people through their moving experience.

Reports to: Sales Manager
Supervises: None
Type: Full-time, salary + commission

Key Responsibilities

Lead Management

  • Respond to inbound leads promptly (calls, web forms, emails)
  • Qualify leads and gather move details
  • Follow up with prospects according to cadence schedule
  • Maintain accurate records in CRM system
  • Hand off qualified leads to estimators when appropriate
  • Track lead sources and conversion metrics

Quoting & Booking

  • Provide accurate pricing based on move details
  • Explain services, pricing structure, and policies clearly
  • Create and send quotes/estimates
  • Handle objections professionally
  • Close bookings and collect deposits
  • Coordinate with dispatch on scheduling

Customer Relationship Building

  • Build rapport with potential customers
  • Listen to customer needs and concerns
  • Provide consultative advice on moving services
  • Follow up after quotes to answer questions
  • Upsell packing services, storage, and add-ons when appropriate
  • Request referrals from satisfied customers

Administrative Duties

  • Maintain CRM with accurate customer data
  • Track daily/weekly/monthly sales metrics
  • Participate in sales meetings and training
  • Collaborate with operations on capacity planning
  • Handle customer service escalations as needed

Requirements

Required Qualifications

  • High school diploma or equivalent
  • 1+ years sales experience (phone or in-person)
  • Excellent verbal and written communication
  • Proficiency with computer systems and CRM software
  • Ability to work in fast-paced environment
  • Professional phone presence

Preferred Qualifications

  • Bachelor's degree
  • Experience in moving, home services, or related industry
  • Bilingual (English/Spanish)
  • Experience with Salesforce, HubSpot, or similar CRM
  • Background in consultative/solution selling

Skills & Competencies

Communication Essential
Active Listening Essential
Persuasion Essential
Organization Essential
Resilience Essential

Performance Metrics

Sales Reps are measured on:

  • Revenue booked: Monthly dollar volume of confirmed jobs
  • Conversion rate: Leads converted to bookings
  • Response time: Speed to first contact with leads
  • Customer feedback: Post-move satisfaction scores
  • Average job value: Including upsells and add-ons

Work Schedule

  • Days: Monday-Friday (some Saturday rotation)
  • Hours: 8 AM - 5 PM typical, some evening availability needed
  • Location: Office-based with potential hybrid options
  • Peak Season: Extended hours March-September

What We Provide

  • Base salary plus uncapped commission
  • CRM and sales tools
  • Ongoing sales training
  • Clear career path and growth opportunities
  • Supportive team environment

Growth Opportunities

Successful Sales Reps can advance to:

  • Senior Sales Representative
  • Sales Team Lead
  • Sales Manager
  • Business Development roles
See also: Sales Compensation for pay details, commission structure, and bonus opportunities.
