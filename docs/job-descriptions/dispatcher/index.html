Dispatcher Job Description

Dispatcher

Position Overview

The Dispatcher is the operational nerve center of Sparrow Moving, responsible for scheduling crews, optimizing routes, managing real-time logistics, and serving as the primary communication hub between field teams, customers, and office staff. This role requires exceptional organizational skills, calm under pressure, and the ability to solve problems quickly.

Reports to: Operations Manager
Classification: Full-time, Non-exempt
Schedule: Monday–Saturday, with rotating weekend coverage

Primary Responsibilities

Scheduling & Crew Assignment

  • Build daily and weekly move schedules based on booked jobs, crew availability, and equipment needs
  • Match crew composition to job requirements (size, complexity, specialty items)
  • Balance workload across crews to prevent burnout and ensure fair distribution
  • Manage call-outs and last-minute staffing changes
  • Coordinate with HR on crew scheduling preferences and time-off requests
  • Maintain minimum staffing levels during peak seasons

Route Optimization

  • Plan efficient routes to minimize drive time and fuel costs
  • Account for traffic patterns, construction, and seasonal factors
  • Sequence multi-stop days for maximum efficiency
  • Adjust routes in real-time based on job progress and delays
  • Monitor GPS tracking to verify crew locations and timing

Real-Time Operations Management

  • Monitor all active jobs throughout the day via dispatch board
  • Respond to field issues: delays, equipment needs, customer concerns
  • Coordinate backup support when jobs run long or encounter problems
  • Make real-time decisions on crew reassignment and schedule adjustments
  • Document incidents, delays, and operational issues as they occur

Communication Hub

  • Serve as primary point of contact between crews and office
  • Send move-day confirmations and arrival windows to customers
  • Update customers on timing changes throughout the day
  • Relay special instructions from estimators to crew leads
  • Communicate with vehicle maintenance when truck issues arise
  • Coordinate with warehouse on storage pickups and deliveries

Equipment & Fleet Coordination

  • Assign trucks and equipment to jobs based on size and requirements
  • Track vehicle maintenance schedules and coordinate repairs
  • Manage equipment inventory (dollies, pads, straps, specialty items)
  • Ensure crews have necessary supplies before leaving yard
  • Report vehicle issues to maintenance and arrange substitutes

Performance Metrics

Dispatchers are evaluated on operational efficiency and customer satisfaction:

Key Performance Indicators

  • On-Time Arrivals: Crews arriving within quoted window (target: 95%+)
  • Schedule Efficiency: Billable hours vs. total scheduled hours (target: 85%+)
  • Route Optimization: Average drive time between jobs minimized
  • Customer Communication: Proactive updates sent before delays impact customers
  • Issue Resolution Time: Field problems addressed within 15 minutes
  • Documentation Quality: Complete and timely dispatch notes

Required Qualifications

  • High school diploma or equivalent
  • 2+ years experience in dispatch, logistics, or operations coordination
  • Strong computer skills with proficiency in scheduling software
  • Excellent multitasking ability and attention to detail
  • Clear verbal and written communication skills
  • Ability to remain calm and make decisions under pressure
  • Knowledge of Twin Cities metro area geography

Preferred Qualifications

  • Experience with moving industry operations or field service dispatch
  • Familiarity with CRM and dispatch software (Chariot, MoveBoard, ServiceTitan)
  • Background in transportation, delivery, or logistics industries
  • Previous experience managing hourly field workers
  • Bilingual (English/Spanish) helpful for crew communication

Essential Skills

Problem-Solving

  • Quick assessment of situations and identification of solutions
  • Creative thinking to resolve scheduling conflicts
  • Ability to anticipate problems before they escalate
  • Judgment to know when to escalate vs. handle independently

Communication

  • Clear, concise instructions to field crews
  • Professional, empathetic communication with customers
  • Timely updates to all stakeholders
  • Effective documentation of decisions and incidents

Organization

  • Managing multiple concurrent priorities without dropping balls
  • Maintaining accurate records and schedules
  • Tracking details across many jobs and crews simultaneously
  • Systematic approach to daily operations

Technical Skills

  • Proficiency with dispatch and scheduling software
  • GPS tracking and route planning tools
  • Multi-line phone systems
  • Basic data entry and reporting

Work Environment

  • Office-based position at Sparrow headquarters
  • Fast-paced environment with constant multi-tasking
  • Extended periods at computer workstation
  • High volume of phone and radio communication
  • May require early morning starts (6:00 AM typical)
  • Occasional overtime during peak moving season (May–September)

A Day in the Life

Here's what a typical day looks like for a Sparrow Dispatcher:

6:00 AM – Arrive at office, review day's schedule, check for overnight changes or call-outs
6:30 AM – Brief crew leads as they arrive, distribute job packets, verify truck assignments
7:00 AM – Crews depart; begin sending customer confirmations with arrival windows
7:30 AM – 12:00 PM – Monitor active jobs, handle field calls, adjust for delays, coordinate support
12:00 PM – Check afternoon job status, plan crew transitions for multi-stop days
1:00 – 5:00 PM – Continue operations monitoring, begin planning next day's schedule
5:00 PM – Verify all crews checked in, document day's issues, finalize tomorrow's assignments

Growth Opportunities

Successful Dispatchers may advance to:

  • Lead Dispatcher overseeing dispatch team and training new hires
  • Operations Manager with broader responsibility for field operations
  • Logistics Coordinator focusing on long-distance and commercial moves
  • Fleet Manager overseeing vehicle maintenance and compliance
