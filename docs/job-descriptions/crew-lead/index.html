Crew Lead

Crew Lead

Position Summary

The Crew Lead is the on-site leader for all moving operations. You'll drive the truck, supervise your crew, ensure customer satisfaction, and represent Sparrow Moving at every job. This role requires a combination of physical capability, leadership skills, and customer service excellence.

Reports to: Operations Manager / Dispatch
Supervises: 1-4 Movers per job
Type: Full-time, hourly

Key Responsibilities

Leadership & Team Management

  • Lead and motivate crew members throughout each job
  • Assign tasks and coordinate workflow for maximum efficiency
  • Train new movers on proper techniques and company standards
  • Conduct pre-job briefings with crew
  • Handle crew conflicts professionally and promptly
  • Complete end-of-job crew evaluations when requested

Customer Relations

  • Serve as primary point of contact with customers on-site
  • Conduct walk-through with customer at start and end of job
  • Address customer concerns and resolve issues in real-time
  • Collect payment and obtain customer signatures
  • Ensure customer satisfaction before leaving the job site
  • Handle complaints professionally and escalate when necessary

Vehicle Operation

  • Drive company trucks safely (16-26 ft box trucks)
  • Perform pre-trip and post-trip vehicle inspections
  • Maintain DOT compliance (if applicable)
  • Navigate to job sites efficiently using GPS/maps
  • Park and position truck for optimal loading/unloading
  • Report any vehicle issues immediately

Moving Operations

  • Protect customer property using proper padding, wrapping, and moving techniques
  • Oversee loading/unloading to ensure proper weight distribution and item security
  • Handle specialty items (pianos, antiques, safes) according to training
  • Disassemble and reassemble furniture as needed
  • Document and photograph any pre-existing damage
  • Maintain inventory lists and paperwork

Administrative Duties

  • Complete all job paperwork accurately
  • Track time and submit timesheets
  • Manage and account for all equipment
  • Communicate with dispatch throughout the day
  • File incident/damage reports when necessary
  • Distribute tips fairly according to policy

Requirements

Required Qualifications

  • Valid driver's license with clean driving record
  • Minimum 1 year experience as a mover (Sparrow or other)
  • Ability to lift 75+ lbs repeatedly
  • Reliable transportation to warehouse
  • Smartphone with data plan
  • Pass background check and drug screening

Preferred Qualifications

  • CDL (or willingness to obtain)
  • Previous supervisory/leadership experience
  • Bilingual (English/Spanish)
  • Experience with specialty items (pianos, antiques)
  • First Aid/CPR certification

Physical Requirements

  • Lift and carry heavy furniture (75-150+ lbs with assistance)
  • Climb stairs repeatedly throughout shift
  • Work in varying weather conditions
  • Stand, walk, bend, and squat for extended periods
  • Work 8-12 hour shifts

Skills & Competencies

Leadership Essential
Customer Service Essential
Problem Solving Essential
Time Management Essential
Communication Essential

Work Schedule

  • Days: Varies based on bookings, typically 5-6 days/week during peak season
  • Hours: Start times vary (usually 6-8 AM), 8-12 hour days common
  • Weekends: Required during busy periods
  • Peak Season: May-September, expect increased hours

Growth Opportunities

Successful Crew Leads may advance to:

  • Senior Crew Lead
  • Training Coordinator
  • Operations Supervisor
  • Branch Manager
