Move Day Checklists

Mover Crew lead

How to Use These Checklists

These checklists ensure consistency and quality across every job. Print them or save to your phone for quick reference. Crew leads should verify completion of each phase before moving to the next.

Pre-Departure Checklist

Complete at the yard before leaving for the job.

Truck Inspection

  • ☐ Walk-around visual inspection completed
  • ☐ Fluid levels checked (oil, coolant, washer)
  • ☐ Tire pressure and condition verified
  • ☐ Lights working (headlights, brake, turn signals, hazards)
  • ☐ Lift gate operational (if equipped)
  • ☐ Interior clean and organized
  • ☐ Pre-trip inspection form signed

    Equipment Load-Out

    • ☐ Moving pads: _____ count (based on job size)
  • ☐ Dollies: 4-wheel, 2-wheel, appliance
  • ☐ Straps: Ratchet straps and E-track fittings
  • ☐ Hand truck / piano board (if needed)
  • ☐ Floor protection: Masonite, plastic runners
  • ☐ Door protection: Door jamb covers
  • ☐ Tool kit: Basic tools, tape, scissors
  • ☐ Specialty equipment for job (crating, safe dolly, etc.)

    Supplies

    • ☐ Packing tape and dispenser
  • ☐ Stretch wrap
  • ☐ Mattress bags (sizes for job)
  • ☐ Labels and markers
  • ☐ Rubber bands for pad securing
  • ☐ Extra boxes (if packing services)
  • ☐ Paper and bubble wrap (if packing services)

    Documentation

    • ☐ Job packet received from dispatch
  • ☐ Customer contact info verified
  • ☐ Origin and destination addresses confirmed
  • ☐ Special instructions reviewed with crew
  • ☐ Specialty items identified
  • ☐ Bill of lading / inventory sheets ready

    Crew Readiness

    • ☐ All crew members present
  • ☐ Uniforms clean and complete
  • ☐ Safety gear on board (gloves, hi-vis vests)
  • ☐ Phones charged
  • ☐ Water on board — hydration reminder given

    • Arrival at Origin

    Complete upon arriving at the pickup location.

    Customer Greeting

    • ☐ Crew lead called customer before arrival with direct phone number
  • ☐ Park truck appropriately (check restrictions)
  • ☐ Introduce yourself and crew to customer
  • ☐ Confirm scope of work — loading plan, fragile items, disassembly needs, items to exclude
  • ☐ Review special instructions or concerns
  • ☐ Identify items NOT being moved
  • ☐ Confirm pets and children will be secured before work starts
  • ☐ Take start time photo (crew at truck)

    Site Assessment

    • ☐ Walk through entire home/space
  • ☐ Identify all items to be moved
  • ☐ Note access challenges (stairs, narrow halls, elevators)
  • ☐ Locate specialty items from estimate
  • ☐ Check for items not on inventory
  • ☐ Photograph any pre-existing damage — text crew lead, upload to CRM with crew visibly prepping in background to timestamp
  • ☐ Note parking/access for destination

    Protection Setup

    • ☐ Lay floor protection on pathways
  • ☐ Install door jamb protection
  • ☐ Protect stair railings if needed
  • ☐ Set up equipment staging area
  • ☐ Position truck ramp/lift gate

    Paperwork

    • ☐ Review and sign Bill of Lading with customer
  • ☐ Confirm valuation coverage selection
  • ☐ Begin inventory documentation
  • ☐ Note pre-existing damage on inventory

    • Loading Phase

    Complete during the loading process.

    Loading Sequence

    • ☐ Start with heavy items to truck front
  • ☐ Mattresses and box springs on walls
  • ☐ Furniture wrapped and padded
  • ☐ Boxes loaded by weight (heavy low, light high)
  • ☐ Fragile items loaded last, near door
  • ☐ Customer essentials accessible

    Quality Checks

    • ☐ All wood surfaces padded
  • ☐ No items touching without protection
  • ☐ Load secured with straps (no shifting)
  • ☐ Weight distributed evenly
  • ☐ Top tier is light items only
  • ☐ No gaps where items could fall

    Specialty Item Handling

    • ☐ Photographed before moving
  • ☐ Extra protection applied
  • ☐ Handling approach communicated to crew
  • ☐ Secured appropriately in truck
  • ☐ Location noted for unloading

    • Origin Departure

    Complete before leaving the pickup location.

    Final Walkthrough

    • ☐ Walk through every room with customer
  • ☐ Check all closets and storage spaces
  • ☐ Check garage, attic, basement
  • ☐ Verify nothing left behind
  • ☐ Customer signs off on origin completion

    Site Cleanup

    • ☐ Remove all protection materials
  • ☐ Collect all equipment and supplies
  • ☐ Pick up any debris or packing materials
  • ☐ Leave site as clean as found
  • ☐ Thank customer, confirm destination ETA

    Pre-Drive Check

    • ☐ Truck door secured and locked
  • ☐ Ramp stowed properly
  • ☐ Lift gate up and secured (if equipped)
  • ☐ Update dispatch on departure
  • ☐ Confirm destination address in GPS

    • Arrival at Destination

    Complete upon arriving at the delivery location.

    Site Setup

    • ☐ Park truck appropriately
  • ☐ Greet customer (or access property)
  • ☐ Walk through destination with customer
  • ☐ Confirm room placement for major items
  • ☐ Note any access challenges
  • ☐ Install floor and door protection

    Unloading

    • ☐ Unload in reverse order (fragile first, heavy last)
  • ☐ Place items per customer direction
  • ☐ Reassemble furniture as quoted
  • ☐ Remove packing materials as you go
  • ☐ Keep pathways clear for safety
  • ☐ Handle specialty items with extra care

    • Job Completion

    Complete before leaving the destination.

    Customer Walkthrough

    • ☐ Walk through every room with customer
  • ☐ Confirm all items delivered
  • ☐ Note any placement adjustments needed
  • ☐ Show customer any items of concern
  • ☐ Address any questions or issues

    Site Cleanup

    • ☐ Remove all floor and door protection
  • ☐ Collect all pads, straps, and equipment
  • ☐ Remove all packing debris
  • ☐ Sweep/clean work areas
  • ☐ Leave property clean and tidy

    Paperwork & Payment

    • ☐ Complete inventory check-off with customer
  • ☐ Document any damage claims
  • ☐ Take end-time photo
  • ☐ Calculate final charges (billed in 15-minute increments after minimum)
  • ☐ Collect payment — cash, check, credit card, or ACH (via Chariot CRM)
  • ☐ Customer signs completion documents
  • ☐ Provide customer copies
  • ☐ Remind customer: tips are 5–20% per mover, paid at final invoice
  • ☐ Request Google review

    Customer Service

    • ☐ Thank customer for choosing Sparrow
  • ☐ Provide claims contact info if issues arose
  • ☐ Remind about review opportunity
  • ☐ Offer referral cards

    • Return to Yard

    Complete when returning to the Sparrow facility.

    Equipment Return

    • ☐ Count and return all pads
  • ☐ Return dollies and equipment to proper location
  • ☐ Return unused supplies
  • ☐ Report any damaged equipment
  • ☐ Report any missing items

    Truck Care

    • ☐ Sweep out truck interior
  • ☐ Remove trash and debris
  • ☐ Report any vehicle issues
  • ☐ Fuel truck if below half tank
  • ☐ Park in designated spot

    Documentation

    • ☐ Turn in job packet and paperwork
  • ☐ Submit any collected payments
  • ☐ Complete time clock / end of day
  • ☐ Report any issues or incidents to dispatch
  • ☐ Debrief with crew lead on job performance

    • Incident Checklists

    If Property Damage Occurs

    • ☐ Stop work and assess the damage
  • ☐ Inform customer immediately
  • ☐ Photograph the damage (multiple angles)
  • ☐ Call dispatch to report
  • ☐ Complete damage documentation form
  • ☐ Get customer signature acknowledging damage
  • ☐ Do NOT admit fault or make repair promises
  • ☐ Give customer claims contact: info@sparrowmovers.com — 48-hour response

    See Claims Protocol for full procedures.

    If Injury Occurs

    • ☐ Stop work and assess the injury
  • ☐ Call 911 if serious — then notify dispatch at (612) 444-1284
  • ☐ Administer first aid if trained and safe (first aid kit is in every truck)
  • ☐ Do not move injured person unless necessary for safety
  • ☐ Document what happened
  • ☐ Get witness information
  • ☐ Take photos of scene
  • ☐ Written incident report submitted within 24 hours

    See Injury & Workers' Comp for full procedures.

    If Vehicle Incident Occurs

    • ☐ Stop vehicle and ensure safety
  • ☐ Check for injuries (call 911 if any)
  • ☐ Do not leave scene
  • ☐ Call police for report
  • ☐ Call dispatch immediately
  • ☐ Exchange info with other party
  • ☐ Photograph all vehicles and scene
  • ☐ Get witness information
  • ☐ Do NOT admit fault

    See Vehicle Policy for full procedures.
