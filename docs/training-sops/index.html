Quick Reference: The Sparrow Flow

Every move follows this basic flow:

  1. Arrival & Walk-Through: Greet customer, assess the job, identify special items
  2. Prep & Protect: Lay floor runners, pad doorways, wrap furniture
  3. Pack: Box remaining items, label everything clearly
  4. Load: Heavy items first, fill gaps, secure load
  5. Transport: Drive safely, communicate with customer about arrival time
  6. Unload: Place items in designated rooms, reassemble furniture
  7. Final Walk-Through: Review placement, check for damage, get sign-off
